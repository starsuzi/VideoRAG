 If you have a swarm of gnats, you know how annoying they can be. In this video, we will tell you how to easily get rid of gnats with a simple home remedy. It is easy. Simply fill a jug with vinegar, add a tin foil lid, and jub several small openings in the cover. Set up traps wherever the gnats are hanging out. The gnats, attracted to the vinegar will go to the container, squirm down into the opening, and won't be able to move out. Have you ever used this home remedy for gnats before?
