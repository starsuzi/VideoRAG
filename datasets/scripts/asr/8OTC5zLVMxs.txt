 For lightweight trenching, the EZ cable installer is ideally suited more the installation and varying of wire for mobile-to-gliding systems or invisible pence-fins. To operate, simply load a reel of selected wire onto the EZ cable installer, thread through the cable guides and secure the loose end into the ground. Just start the engine, tilt the machine back to clear the blade, engage the cutting lever and lower it into the ground while pulling gently. Notice how easily it cuts the trench, installs the wire and backfills all in one convenient operation. With its all steel construction and carbide tip blade, the EZ cable installer will cut through the toughest soils and is ideal for cutting straight or curved shallow trenches. The EZ cable installer makes easy work of wiring installation for invisible pence-fins and for low voltage lighting systems. This portable lightweight trencher is easily loaded and unloaded by one person and will fit in a medium sized car trunk.
