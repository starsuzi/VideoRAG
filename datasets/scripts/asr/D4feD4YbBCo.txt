 Rubber plant is a popular indoor house plant. Their leaves are very attractive. Slim and tall rubber plants are good for rooms with high ceiling. A bushy rubber plant looks good on a table or self. Dead leaves and branches cap be prune day anytime. Best time to prune is from spring to summer, for pruning you will need clean sharp pruning shears. Also add gloves to protect your skin from the sap. Cut the rubber plants just above anode, that is at the leaf joint. New branches will emerge at the cuts. Plant cut branches in free draining soil to make new rubber plants. When the branches grow two-three sets of leaves, cut again above the node. This way the plant will grow many side branches, making the plant bushy. Please like the video and subscribe to my channel.
