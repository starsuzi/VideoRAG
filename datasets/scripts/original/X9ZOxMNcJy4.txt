care instructions for your sobo hoodie
you can wash the edison hoodie like any
other piece of apparel but there are a
couple of considerations
you'll want to remove the battery pack
from the inside pocket prior to washing
open up the hoodie open the velcro
pocket
pull out the battery pack place the
connectors between your thumb and
forefingers
pressing down on the tabs and
disconnecting the connectors
wash the hoodie hang dry only reconnect
the connectors place
back in the pocket and seal the velcro
zip up your hoodie
turn on the light and head out we
recommend placing the battery button
towards the outside pocket that way when
you put your hand in the pocket you're
able to turn on the light
