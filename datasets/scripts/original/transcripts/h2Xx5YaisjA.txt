This tutorial will guide you through the daily
care of your Lattissima One machine.
Do not use any strong cleaning
agent or solvent cleaner.
Use a clean damp cloth and mild cleaning
agent to clean the surface of the machine.
Do not put any machine components in dishwasher, except the Rapid Cappuccino System components.
Lift and close the lever to eject the capsule into the used capsule container.
Remove the drip tray and capsule container
Empty and rinse them.
Then, remove the water tank.
Empty it, rinse it
and fill it with potable water.
Place a container under the coffee outlet and press the Lungo button to rinse.
Finish by cleaning the coffee outlet and the machine
surface with a clean damp cloth.
Thank you for watching this tutorial.
