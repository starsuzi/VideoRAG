Hello Everyone. Today I will be showing you how you can easily make Chole Bhature at home easily
Lets make the dough for Bhature first. The exact measurements are given in the description box
Make a dough with little lukewarm water
Add water little by little
Now take a wet kitchen towel or a cotton cloth and cover the dough and keep it for good 4-5 hours in a hot place like microwave or oven
Now lets start preparing chikpea curry or CHOLE.. recipe mentioned in the description box
Now our dough is ready after 4-5 hours . Just roll them like paratha or roti. and fry them
