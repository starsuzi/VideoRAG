to check the refrigerator for proper
door closure open the doors at a slight
angle let go of the doors and they
should close
automatically if they do not then note
the adjustment of the front leveling
legs they can be turned clockwise or
counterclockwise to adjust the
refrigerator slightly up or down to
provide for proper door closure
the rollers in the rear are stationary
wheels and are not adjustable but do
provide for ease of rolling the
refrigerator in and out of
place also take note of the condenser
and make sure that there is no dirt
buildup as a dirty condenser can affect
overall temperatures
